Module 6 · Activity 1 of 3

What Would "Working" Look Like?

⏱ About 5 minutes

Before thinking about how to get there, imagine the destination. One year from now, your idea has been running. What do you see? Defining success before you start is one of the most underrated skills in social innovation — and most people skip it.

✅ What would you see, hear, or feel that tells you your idea is working?
Be specific — not "it would be successful" but what that actually looks like in practice
⚠️ What would tell you it isn't working?
What are the warning signs — the things you'd see or not see if the idea wasn't having the impact you hoped?
💡 The gap between these two answers is where your measurement and learning strategy lives. You'll come back to this in the planning canvas.
