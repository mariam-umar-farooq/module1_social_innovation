Module 3 · Activity 3 of 3

Whose Voice Is Missing?

⏱ About 5–7 minutes

Stakeholder mapping is only useful if you're honest about what you're not seeing. These reflection questions are designed to surface the blind spots.

😰 Which stakeholder are you most nervous to approach — and why?
🧠 Whose perspective have you been assuming without actually asking?
Be honest — we all make assumptions about what people want or need
🔄 What would change about your idea if the most affected people had designed it themselves?
💬 Peer prompt: Share your stakeholder map with someone and ask: "Who am I not seeing?" Note what they said below.
